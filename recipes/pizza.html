Pizza

Description

Ever wondered how to make such an amazing dish? Well today is you're lucky day because here we will walk you through everything you will need and the steps to take to make such an amazing dish! It is very simple to make so don't get frustrated! With our simple steps, we will accomplish this!

Ingridients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Any Topping Of You're Choice
  1. Make dough flat with crust at the edges
  2. Add tomato sauce and spread evenly around the dough
  3. Add cheese on top of tomato sauce
  4. Add toppings of you're choice on top of cheese
  5. Place pizza inside of pre-heated oven and let it cook!
  6. Keep checking on pizza until fully cooked
